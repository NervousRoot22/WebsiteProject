Welcome

If you long for the flavors of Russia, you’ll find honest, homestyle goodness at the Cinderella Bakery & Café. If you’ve never tasted Russian cooking before, you’re in for a tasty surprise.

Open Daily 7 am - 7 pm

We’re located in the Richmond district between 5th and 6th Avenues on Balboa Street. Visit us to pick up irresistible treats, order lunch, or grab a bite to take to Golden Gate Park.
